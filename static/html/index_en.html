Guaranteed acceptance 

Our operator promises the investor a minimum return on the principal. The commitment will be fulfilled in any case. 

The operator charges risk reserves based on each loan’s volume, in order to hedge the exposure when the value of collateral cannot cover the loan amount. 

Introduce specialized third-party assets management companies to be the ultimate risk takers.

Step 1

Borrower applies for loan 

Loan details page: borrowers need to complete a loan application to provide details on loan and collateral; and more information, like service fee, is shown on the right of the page.

Step 2

Loan request released, open for investment

After borrowers confirm the application, the loan request will be listed on the Invest page; and lenders can fund their selected loan based on the collateral type, yields and other factors. 

 >

Step 3

Borrowing successful

The loan amount will be sent to the borrower’s digital account as soon as the lender complete the investment.

Step 4

Repay on schedule 

Sufficient capital is required in the borrower’s digital account when the due date comes; and by clicking “Repayment” in the figure below, borrowers can pay back the capital instantly.
